Verification System

Three checks. One verdict.

Each card carries three data points. Read all three before you judge.

FMV Oracle

Queries the Renaiss price index for the card's Fair Market Value. If the ask price is more than 2.5× the FMV — it's suspect.

$1,200 FMV — CONFIRMED
$12,000 ask / $400 FMV — SUSPECT
SBT Provenance

Verifies the Soul-Bound Token chain-of-custody. A missing or zeroed owner address means no provenance — reject it.

0xAb3…f29 — VERIFIED
0x000…0000 — MISSING
Vault Multi-Sig

Checks the vault custody threshold. PSA 8+ cards need 3-of-5 signatures. Below 8 or flagged drops to 1-of-5 — insecure.

4-of-5 — SECURED
1-of-5 — INSECURE
